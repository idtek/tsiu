UDT: UDP-based Data Transfer Library - version 3

Yunhong Gu

Welcome to the UDT3 SDK documentation.

UDT is also the name of our high performance data transfer protocol - UDP-based data transfer protocol. It is designed for data intensive applications over wide area networks such as computational grids, to overcome the efficiency and fairness problems of TCP.

Visit http://udt.sf.net for most recent news on UDT.

Check out most current UDT release at SourceForge or from CVS.

export CVS_RSH=ssh

cvs -d:pserver:anonymous@cvs.sourceforge.net:/cvsroot/udt login

[NOTE: when prompt for password, press the RETURN/ENTER key]

cvs -d:pserver:anonymous@cvs.sourceforge.net:/cvsroot/udt co UDT3
